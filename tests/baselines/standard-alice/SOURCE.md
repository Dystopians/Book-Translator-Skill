# standard-alice

- File: `standard-alice.epub`
- Source: Standard Ebooks edition of *Alice's Adventures in Wonderland*, with
  illustrations by John Tenniel.
- Source page:
  <https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/lewis-carroll/alices-adventures-in-wonderland/john-tenniel>
- Purpose: an image-rich, chaptered regression input for conversion, internal
  links, illustration paths, recurring entities, merge order, and final EPUB,
  DOCX, HTML, and PDF generation.

At the default target size of 6000 characters, a clean conversion should
produce roughly 38 chunks and retain the illustration set. Exact counts should
be compared against the generated manifest rather than assumed by a worker.
